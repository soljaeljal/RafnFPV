Red Bull Karen

About the project

This project was meant as a supplement to a larger production by Redbull. I was asked to come up with a dynamic scene for a short bit leading into the reveal of the event car, which lead me to improvise when i saw the windmill on the way to the location that the event took place. The sequence starts with a quick manuever around the blade of the windmill leading into a reveal of the car, a dynamic shot from the side, and lastly into a break-away leading into a birds eye view of the car.

Gear used

The rig used for this project was my custom high-speed setup with a 4k action camera, and a battery with lower capacity to allow for quicker maneuverability. Having the overall sequence be short and consisting of a single take, posed no issue for the low battery capacity that might have been an issue on another job.
