Maersk Panum

About the project

For this shoot I was commissioned by Maersk to take a cinematic panorama of their building. The intention was for this to be used in a commercial so they really wanted it to be of high quality and to really be able to see every frame. I went through the plan with the client and laid out the route that I was going take for this shoot, the resulting product was this short clip which was then partially used for their commercial.

Gear used

This project was carried out using one of my custom drones for cinematic use, the drone was fitted with a DSLR and the footage was stabilized in post.
