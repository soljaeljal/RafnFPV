Padborg Park

About the project

This project was commissioned by the Park to serve as a cinematic action sequence for a promotional video that they were producing. My role was to capture various cars on the track in a dynamic fashion. I was also asked to take some aerial shots to show the park itself.

Gear used

The rig used for this project was my custom high-speed setup with a 4k action camera, and a battery with lower capacity to allow for quicker maneuverability, this also meant that i had to have replacements ready as the flight time suffered as a result.
